Learn how it works

As native landscapes have been cleared and simplified, our cities have lost biodiversity, canopy, soils, and water — yet every garden still holds the potential to restore what was lost.

What Yield Is

Yield is the annual return you receive when your garden delivers real ecological outcomes.

It's not an offset. It's not a donation.

Yield is recognition of value you're already creating — and an incentive to create more.

Sponsors fund gardens that deliver real, local ecological outcomes. You receive that support year after year, as long as those outcomes continue.

How It Works

1. Discover

Find out what ecological value your garden already produces.

2. Improve

Work with your garden to increase habitat, canopy, or pollinator support — at your own pace.

3. Activate

Choose to make your garden's outcomes available to sponsors seeking local ecological impact.

4. Receive

Receive annual income or in-kind support while your garden continues to perform.

What Can Generate Yield

Habitat

Indigenous plants that support local bird, insect, and animal species.

Pollinators

Flowering natives that sustain bees, butterflies, and other essential species.

Canopy

Trees and shrubs that provide measurable shade, cooling, and carbon storage.

Cooling

Vegetation that reduces urban heat and improves neighbourhood climate.

Soil

Healthy, living ground cover that supports water retention and biodiversity below the surface.

For Homeowners

Designed to be simple, voluntary, and low-risk.

  • No upfront cost to participate
  • Full control and privacy retained
  • Annual income or support while ecological outcomes are maintained
  • Better garden outcomes through design guidance and plant selection
  • Your property remains entirely yours

For Sponsors

Designed for real, place-based ecological impact.

  • Real, verifiable ecological outcomes in specific neighbourhoods
  • Transparency through independent verification systems
  • Direct connection to the places and people you're supporting
  • Tangible impact, not abstract offsets or credits
  • Long-term relationships with measurable results
Trust & Verification

Every garden that generates Yield is independently verified through The Ecological Registry.

Outcomes are measured against Registry standards and recorded annually. Your address and personal details remain private. Participation is voluntary and can be paused or ended at any time.

You decide what information is shared, and with whom.
